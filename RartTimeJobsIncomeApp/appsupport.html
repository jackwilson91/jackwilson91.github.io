Part-Time Income version:1.0

This is a part-time job income record app that records your part-time job information and can also record your part-time income information.

1. Home:

The home page displays your income graph, which allows you to view all part-time income, all part-time income for the current year, part-time income for the month, part-time income for the previous month, and view the current year's income pie chart by type of part-time job.


2. Jobs:

You can create your part-time job, or you can record revenue information and view revenue list information based on part-time creation.


3. Income:

You can add revenue information when you choose your part-time job, or you can view all of the revenue amount.


4. More:

You can export revenue information for all your part-time jobs, and the exported file is a spreadsheet.
